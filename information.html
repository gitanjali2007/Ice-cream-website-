VANILLA ICE-CREAM

Vanilla ice cream is a classic, creamy dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla flavoring (extract, beans, or artificial), known for its versatility as a base for other desserts like sundaes or milkshakes, with nutrition varying by brand but generally containing fats, carbs, and sugar, and available in natural or artificially flavored versions.

CHOCOLATE ICE-CREAM

Chocolate ice cream is a classic frozen dessert made by adding cocoa powder, chocolate liquor, or melted chocolate to a standard ice cream base of cream, sugar, and eggs, offering a rich, bittersweet, or sweet flavor and brown color, and it's one of the original ice cream flavors, existing even before vanilla.

STRAWBERY

Strawberry ice cream is a beloved, nostalgic dessert made from cream, sugar, eggs (sometimes), and real strawberries, offering a balance of sweet and tangy flavors, popular across generations for its classic taste, often featuring fresh fruit chunks for authenticity

BLUE BERRY

Blueberry ice cream is a popular frozen dessert blending creamy dairy (milk, cream, sugar) with tangy, sweet blueberry puree or sauce, offering a vibrant flavor and smooth texture, often with swirls of fruit.
